About Myself

I'm Iftekhar Anam, a Software Engineering graduate from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology with a strong passion for AI research. Currently, I'm pursuing opportunities for a PhD to advance my research career in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

As a Research Intern at the Advanced Machine Intelligence Research Lab (AMIR Lab), I've published first-author papers on UAV-based precision agriculture and lightweight convolutional neural networks and presented a paper at an international conference. My research interests focus on medical image analysis, explainable AI, computer vision, and AI/ML applications.

I'm currently developing XAD-Net, an ensemble framework for Alzheimer's classification with explainable visual insights, which reflects my commitment to creating interpretable AI solutions for healthcare. My technical expertise spans AI development tools like TensorFlow and Keras, alongside frontend development experience from my internship at Kaz Software Limited.

My goal is to pursue a research-focused career where I can contribute to advancing AI technology while solving real-world problems through innovative, explainable machine learning solutions.
